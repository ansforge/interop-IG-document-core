Logical Model: CDA - patientRole

Official URL: https://interop.esante.gouv.fr/ig/document/core/StructureDefinition/fr-cda-patient-role Version: 0.1.0
Draft as of 2026-01-26 Computable Name: FRCDAPatientRole
